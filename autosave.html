WORK • AUTOSAVE CASE STUDY






USER PROBLEM


Losing meal selection progress

37% of HelloFresh users have lost their meal selection progress because they have left the app without saving their meals. This pain point is experienced by users at all loyalty levels and particularly those with larger basket sizes.

During user interviews evualuating a meal customisation feature 65% of participants failed to discover the “Save” button even after being prompted to “fully save” their changes, but felt their changes had been automatically saved nonetheless.

Receiving an incorrect or unexpected order

14% of users expect that they will not receive a box until they complete meal selection, when in fact they will automatically receive box - either a default selection or a wrong order.
REVIEW OF EXISTING DATA


User feedback

300 complaints/month through Usabilla about the inability to save selections and the ultimate mismatch between their order and what they receive.

66% of users prefer to select their meals on a mobile device.

Competitors

4/5 direct meal kit competitors have an autosave feature.
