GOALGIRLS Member Database
GOALGIRLS CO-CREAGENCY



Tools: Sketch, Wix Corvid


Deliverable: Member Database & Landing Page


Time Span: May 2020 - ongoing


Team: Amanda Low

Background

GOALGIRLS is a Berlin creative agency, founded in 2017 by Helena and Kaddie Rothe. Their focus is female driven and culturally aware projects with brands such as Bumble, Netflix, Casper, and ARMEDANGELS in their portfolio.

In 2019 they launched the CO-CREAGENCY community - a talent pool of over 100 freelance female creatives, working in an agile structure to generate innovative ideas for purpose-driven advertising campaigns.

Problem
Problem: Manual Process

The CO-CREAGENCY needed to showcase it's biggest asset: The talent pool of female creatives female creatives. Although the selection process was done with care, there was not yet a way for outsiders to view who the CO-CREAGENCY members, and what their skillsets, were.

All requests for member information had to be handled by the community manager - a manual process.
Task: Online Database

Design and create a working template for a members database, for up to 150 members.

With a online database, the goal would be:
  • Provide visiblity for the individual CO-CREAGENCY members
  • Showcase the members' skillsets
  • Help to recruit new members
The database should be searchable, and have an easily updatable template. The CO-CREAGENCY were inspired by a yearbook, however were open to all suggestions for the layout and functionality of the database.

As the website used the Wix CMS the database would have to fit within the parameters of what was possible there.
Research
Survey


The aim of the survey was to gain information from the CO-CREAGENCY talent pool regarding:
  • How the member database could help them
  • What information they would be willing to share
  • Their involvement with the CO-CREAGENCY
The key takeaways from the survey were:
  • 85% saw seeing other members' skillsets as beneficial to them
  • The top three features that members' thought were most important were: Name, Skills, and Photograph
  • Less important features to include were Biography, Social Media, and Website URL
Suggestions for other features to include were: a fun fact, and vision.

After discussing the survey results with the CO-CREAGENCY, it was decide to emphasise the members' skillsets, by adding a filter to narrow down the members shown by selecting a certain skill.
Competitive Analysis


In order to get a better idea of what the CO-CREAGENCY would like for the format, I created a competitive analysis for them. Similar niche agencies in Berlin I researched did not have a member database, so I had to look internationally for examples.

While I researched both traditional and creative competitor layout styles, the final layout would be suject to the limitations of the Wix tool.

Stakeholder feedback on the comptitive analysis included that the name should be below the photo, and no text should cover the photo. Preferred layout was 3 member cards per row.
CMS
Database Functionality


In order to create an updatable template, I wanted to create a spreadsheet where member data could be entered, exported as a CSV file, then imported into the Wix Database. After testing with placeholders I found that text based fields were easily imported via spreadsheet into Wix, however to import images via spreadsheet, they needed to be hosted externally. As the website images wouuld be hosted on Wix, the photos would need to be uploaded and assigned manually.

In order to create the "filter by skill" functionality, I employed Wix Corvid - an open development platform for adding JavaScript functionality to websites.
Style


Colour palette and fonts were already set by the CO-CREAGENCY brand guide. I kept the design simple, as the main task was to first test the functionality of the database. Plus, final adjustments to the design were still TBC by the CO-CREAGENCY creative director.
