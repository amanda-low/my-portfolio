About
I'm currently a full-time UX Designer at Afilio. Previously, I've worked as a freelance UX Designer at GOALGIRLS creative agency, and in digital marketing for e-commerce stores, including Horizn Studios, Glossybox, and nu3.

I'm proficient in designing and prototyping with Figma, Sketch, and Adobe CC among others. I write HTML & CSS with confidence and love finding data-driven solutions for complex problems. From my experience working in fashion, beauty, and wellness e-commerce I bring a solid knowledge of conversion optimisation, data analysis, and user journey.

Originally from Adelaide in South Australia, I moved to Berlin in 2011 seeking adventure and am now a German permanent resident (ich spreche auch Deutsch!). In my spare time, I enjoy yoga, coding, and writing.
