About

Amanda's work lives at the intersection of digital products and human experiences. In her studio and through her research, she crafts designs that feel intuitive and meaningful.

With a background in both product and service design, she brings a holistic perspective to every project. She believes the best designs emerge from deeply understanding user needs, business goals, and the broader ecosystem in which a product exists.

When she's not designing, you'll find her exploring new cities, sketching in coffee shops, or diving into a good book about behavioral psychology.
