About

I'm currently a full-time UX Designer at Afilio. Previously, I've worked as a freelance UX Designer at GOALGIRLS creative agency, and have 8 years of experience creating marketing experiences for e-commerce stores, including Horizn Studios, Glossybox, and nu3.

Originally from Adelaide in South Australia, I moved to Berlin in 2011 seeking adventure and am now a German permanent resident. In my spare time, I enjoy yoga, coding, and writing.
