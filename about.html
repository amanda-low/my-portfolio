ABOUT ME



I've been a Product Designer at HelloFresh in Berlin since 2020. Previously, I've worked as a UX designer at Afilio, and a freelance UX Designer at GOALGIRLS creative agency. In addition I have extensive e-commerce experience - 8 years creating marketing experiences for in travel, beauty, and health for Horizn Studios, Glossybox, and nu3.

Originally from Adelaide in South Australia, I moved to Berlin in 2011 seeking adventure and am now a German citizen. In my spare time, I enjoy yoga, contemporary fiction, and searching flea markets for mid-century furniture.

I'm inspired by sustainability and regeneration, and minimalist and functionalist design.
