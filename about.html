About

I'm currently a freelance UX Designer at GOALGIRLS creative agency in Berlin. Previously I've worked for Horizn Studios, a direct to consumer luxury travel brand, Glossybox, a beauty subscription service, and nu3, a health and fitness retailer, as a team leader and digital marketing specialist.

Originally from Adelaide in South Australia, I moved to Berlin in 2011 seeking adventure, and am now a German permanent resident (ich spreche auch Deutsch!).

Side projects including writing articles for Ground Magazine, and learning new coding languages (currently working on JavaScript).

I'm passionate about sustainability and love learning about emerging technologies that lead to more efficient, environmentally sustainable solutions.

I'm currently searching for new opportunities. Please feel free to reach out for a chat!
