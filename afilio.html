Problem

Afilio has successfully launched two Care Level Tools (Calculator and Application Funnels) however there is no way for users to track the progress of their application. A portal is needed to give an overview of the process and provide information on the next steps.

Our task was to design a portal that allowed users to view their results from the Care Level Tools, make necessary adjustments, and be guided through what is normally a lengthy, bureaucratic process.

I was responsible for research, concept development, wireframing, implementation, A/B testing, data analysis, and user testing. I worked with Johanna Reinert, Jacqueline Abel, Robert Bausmert, and Philip Harms on this project.
Problem Statement

Overwhelmed family members need to quickly and easily apply for a care level in order to focus on the care of the person in need.
