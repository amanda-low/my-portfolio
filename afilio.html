Problem

Afilio has successfully launched two Care Level Tools (Calculator and Application Funnels) however there is no way for users to track the progess of their application. A portal is needed to give an overview of the process and provide information on the next steps.

Our task was to design a portal that allowed users to view their results from the Care Level Tools, make necessary adjustments, and be guided through what is normally a lengthy, bureaucratic process.

I worked alongside Johanna Reinert, Jacqueline Abel, Robert Bausmert, and Philip Harms on the research, wireframing, UI, analysis, testing, and reiteration.
Problem Statement

Overwhelmed family members need to quickly and easily apply for a care level in order to focus on the care of the person in need.
